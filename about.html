About Me

Thank you for checking out my bio page. This is a place where I will continue to showcase my Web Development skills. Over the past few years, I've been fortunate enough to work with some very brilliant people who have inspired me to pursue my passion for Web Development. My journey took me from working in the Mortgage Industry, to working as a Contractor at MS, to now being a Technical PM for a Consulting Agency. My hope is that through the skills I learn, in this Bootcamp, I will find not only a job but a career.

I have several passions that keep me busy outside of work and school. First and foremost, I have 2 young daughters who mean the world to me. We are a loving family filled with lots of laughter and lots of love. When I'm not hanging with the family, I enjoy watching Sports. I especially love watching Baseball and Basketball. Ever since I was a little kid growing up in the Bay Area, I have loved Baseball. My Grandfather used to take my Brother and me up to SF to see the Giants or A's There's nothing better than going to a Baseball Game in Summer. Now that I'm grown and have a family, I get to share that passion with my girls who love going to Baseball Games.

I'm also an avid College Basketball Fan. Love my UW Huskies and UNC Tar Heels. While I was sad to see Coach Romar go, I think it was time. Sometimes you have to inject new blood to get things moving again. That's what UW has done with Coach Hop. In 2 short years, he's taken them from a completely irrelevant Team to the top of the Pac-12 Conference two years in a row. Most recently, they have ended an eight year drought of appearances in the NCAA Tournament. He's got a great recruiting class coming in for next year too!

My most favorite Team though, is UNC. I grew up watching the likes of Kenny Smith, Brad Daugherty, Jerry Stackhouse, et al. One year for an early Birthday present, my wife bought us two tickets to NC to go watch the Tar Heels play. Sadly I wasn't able to see them beat Duke but we got a win nonetheless! In addition to the Tar Heels, I also enjoy watching the Huskies play Basketball. Each Summer, my Brother and I take time during the week to go watch the local HS Kids and incoming Freshmen play Summer League Ball. You really get to see some great Basketball!
